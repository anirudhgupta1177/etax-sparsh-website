Our story

Tax filing, done the way it should be

eTax Profilling began with a simple frustration: filing taxes in India was either intimidatingly complex or worryingly careless. We set out to fix that - pairing real Chartered Accountants with smart AI so every taxpayer, from a first-time earner to a growing business, files accurately, claims every rupee they’re owed, and never loses sleep over compliance.

Why we exist

Built by a Chartered Accountant, for every taxpayer

Our mission is to make professional, accurate tax filing accessible and affordable to everyone in India - not just those who can afford a private CA on retainer.

For too long, good tax advice sat behind high fees and confusing jargon, while low-cost software left people guessing and overpaying. We bridge that gap. Every return is prepared with the rigour of a qualified CA and the speed of intelligent automation - so nothing is missed, nothing is overpaid, and nothing is left to chance.

We’re obsessive about three things: accuracy that holds up to scrutiny, transparency in what you pay and why, and refunds - making sure you keep every rupee the law allows. That obsession is the reason families, freelancers and founders across 2,600+ towns trust us with their most sensitive numbers.

How we got here

Our journey

From a solo CA practice in Gwalior to a nationwide tax platform - built one accurate return at a time.

2019 - Founded in Gwalior

CA Sparsh Gupta started eTax Profilling in Gwalior with one belief: professional tax help shouldn’t be a luxury. The first returns were filed for local families and shopkeepers.

2020 - Expert-assisted filing goes online

We launched fully remote, expert-assisted ITR filing - letting anyone in India connect with a dedicated CA, share documents securely, and file without ever visiting an office.

2022 - Crossed 1 lakh returns

Word spread well beyond Madhya Pradesh. Salaried professionals, investors and freelancers across metros and small towns pushed us past our first 1,00,000 filed returns.

2024 - Launched our AI tax assistant

We rolled out an AI assistant trained on Indian tax law to auto-import AIS & 26AS, surface missed deductions and compare regimes - with a human CA always verifying before filing.

2025 - Expanded to GST & business compliance

Responding to founders and growing firms, we added GST registration and returns, TDS, bookkeeping and startup compliance - becoming a full back office for Indian businesses.

2026 - 12 lakh+ returns filed nationwide

Today we serve taxpayers across 2,600+ towns and cities with a 4.9★ rating - and we’re just getting started on making accurate, honest tax filing the norm in India.
